About

Jack Abaza is a philosopher, writer, and provocateur of order’s illusions. His work examines the ruins beneath reason, the shadow behind revelation, and the primal serpent coiled beneath all thought.

Book

The Definitive Answer to the Meaning of Life (Wipf & Stock, 2023) is a brutal critique of the axioms we dare not question. Abaza dissects morality, aesthetics, knowledge, and consciousness in a style that’s unapologetically sovereign.

Available in paperback, hardcover, Kindle, and ebook formats.
