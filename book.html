The Definitive Answer to the Meaning of Life

The Definitive Answer to the Meaning of Life is the last philosophical investigation of a question that has haunted humanity for millennia. Unlike the endless theorizing of past philosophers, this work doesn’t begin with answers—it begins with correcting the question itself.

Since the Analects, thinkers have repeatedly failed to pause and ask: “What does it even mean to ask for the meaning of life?” This book takes that fundamental oversight seriously. Rather than layering new answers on top of old errors, it redefines the question from first principles—dissecting assumptions about morality, consciousness, knowledge, and more.

The result is a work that claims what no philosophy book has before: the question is answerable—and this book answers it. A free preview (Preface and Chapter 1) is available on Amazon. You decide whether the quality speaks for itself.
