The Definitive Answer to the Meaning of Life

The Definitive Answer to the Meaning of Life is the final philosophical investigation of humanity’s oldest question. Unlike past thinkers, Abaza begins not with answers—but with a piercing challenge to the way the question itself has been framed.

Since the Analects, not one philosopher paused to ask: “What does it mean to ask for the meaning of life?” That omission—born of intellectual pride—has led to millennia of error. This book dismantles that illusion by starting from first principles.

The result is a fierce dissection of the axioms behind morality, consciousness, knowledge, and aesthetic judgment. What emerges is a conclusion no philosopher dared claim: the question is answerable, and here—finally—is the answer.
